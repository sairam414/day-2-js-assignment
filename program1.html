JavaScript console.time() Method

Press F12 on your keyboard to view the result in the console view.

Time the number of milliseconds it takes to perform a for-loop a hundred thousand times.

Note: the console.time() method starts the timer and the console.timeEnd() method ends the timer and writes the result in the console view.

Press F12 on your keyboard to view the result in the console view.
